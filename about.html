About Agape Secondary School

Empowering the Next Generation

Agape Secondary School is a part of the arm of Education Department of Agape Life Church, Chilomoni Branch. It is a day secondary school located in Blantyre, Chilomoni Nthukwa.

Our History

Agape Secondary School was established in 2019 with the goal of providing quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and character development. From humble beginnings with only two classrooms and a small teaching staff, the school has grown into a vibrant institution serving hundreds of students each year. Over the years, Agape has consistently achieved outstanding results in national examinations while also equipping learners with critical life skills.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and strong moral principles.
  • Excellence: Striving for the best in all areas of learning and conduct.
  • Discipline: Encouraging self-control and responsibility.
  • Respect: Valuing diversity and treating others with dignity.
  • Faith: Building lives on strong Christian foundations.

Mission

Founded with a passion for excellence and guided by the values of love, respect, and integrity, our school provides a nurturing environment where students can grow intellectually, morally, and socially.

We offer a balanced curriculum that combines strong academic programs, co-curricular activities, and life skills training. Our dedicated teachers are committed to bringing out the best in every learner, encouraging critical thinking, creativity, and a spirit of service to the community.

At Agape Secondary School, every student matters. We foster a culture where discipline meets compassion, ensuring that learners are not only prepared for examinations, but also for life beyond the classroom.
