About Agape Secondary School

Agape Secondary School is a part of the arm of Education Department of Agape Life Church, Chilomoni Branch. It is a day secondary school located in Blantyre, Chilomoni Nthukwa.

WHY AGAPE?

Agape Secondary School is a place where learners are nurtured to become responsible, compassionate, and well-rounded individuals. Our name, Agape, reflects our commitment to love and service in education.

As a registered MANEB Centre, discipline and academic excellence are at the forefront of our mission.
